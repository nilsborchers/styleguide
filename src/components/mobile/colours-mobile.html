Mobile Colours

Colors on mobile devices has a much more limited range than on desktop and are more similar to the APP.

Primary Colours

Our base colour palette is monochrome.
  • $colorDarkPrimary
  • $colorLightPrimary

Additional Colours

Only 3 shades of grey
  • $colorLightSecondary
  • $colorMedium
  • $colorDarkTertiary

Feedback/Highlight Colours

These colours are used to highlight elements and/or to give positive, negative or neutral feedback.
  • $colorPositiveDark
  • $colorPositiveLight
  • $colorNeutral
  • $colorNegativeDark
  • colorNegativeLight
