Price Box

Price boxes are a common way to display a price of an item. Note the containers for reduced and special prices:

$65
$80
$10 
<div class="price-box">
  <div class="price-box__regular-price">
    <span>$65</span>
  </div>
  <div class="price-box__old-price">
    <span>$80</span>
  </div>
  <div class="price-box__special-price">
    <span>$10</span>
  </div>
</div>

This also works really nice with the font size helper classes:

$80
$10 
<div class="price-box fsxxl">
  <div class="price-box__old-price">
    <span>$80</span>
  </div>
  <div class="price-box__special-price">
    <span>$10</span>
  </div>
</div>

Titlebar

Titlebars

This component is used in many areas of the shop, for example:

  • On my account pages
  • To host breadcrumps
  • In the catalog to show the number of search results

Titlebars are used in conjunction with the spacing helper classes.

Hi there! I'm a title-bar. 
<div class="title-bar pal">
    Hi there! I'm a title-bar.
</div>
